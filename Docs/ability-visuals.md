# Ability Visual & Thematic Descriptions

## Auto-Attack — "Homing Round" *(Both Classes)*
A glowing projectile locks onto an enemy and tracks them across the battlefield. The shooter's body pulses with a white glow as they wind up the shot, then the bolt arcs forward and homes in on its target.

---

## Tank

### Q — "Heavy Round"
A large, slow-moving energy orb fires from the Tank's position toward a target point. It travels in a straight line and hits like a cannonball — wide, deliberate, and hard to miss.

### W — "Bulwark"
The Tank is encased in three shimmering rings of blue energy that orbit their body. The rings glow with a cool, oceanic light, absorbing incoming damage before it reaches the Tank's health.

### E — "Buckshot"
Five glowing orbs orbit the Tank at high speed as they charge up, then burst outward in a wide fan arc. A purple trajectory line shows the spread, and a circular range indicator marks how far the shot will reach.

---

## Ranger

### Q — "Swift Shot"
A quick, compact projectile fires toward a target point. Smaller and faster than the Tank's orb, it travels in a straight line with a clean snap — precise and responsive.

### W — "Blink Step"
A green arrow appears at the Ranger's feet, pointing in their dash direction. On release, the Ranger vanishes and reappears ahead in a burst of motion — low to the ground, quick as a reflex.

### E — "Burst Fire"
Three glowing orbs orbit the Ranger while charging. On release, they fire in rapid sequence along the same line — a tight burst of shots down a single corridor. An orange range ring and a light-blue trajectory line show where they'll land.
